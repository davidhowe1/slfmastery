WATCH USMAN IN ACTION

''growing up, i always felt I was not good enough''

As the youngest of 4 siblings, the son of a verbally abusive father and an overworked suffering mother, I grew up with extremely low self esteem and weak emotional boundaries. Short and fat, that’s what my dad would call me as a child, and I believed him. I came to beleve that I was small, unimportant, not attractive and not ‘good enough’. I became a people pleaser, avoiding rejection at any cost, and seeing my mother struggle left me feeling somewhat responsible for her difficulty and pain.

''feeling weak and constantly rejected were the best things that ever happened to me''

I grew tired of feeling so inadequate. Having my appearance made fun of, being paralysed with lack of confidence around the opposite sex, developing an abusive relationship with food, to being plagued by anxiety and intrusive thoughts filling my mind from wake to sleep, and then developing Inflammatory Bowel Disease (Ulcerative Colitis)… I finally came to realize that these were all self-created experiences, and therefore I had the key to my own freedom and growth.

USMANS TIMELINE

''become comfortable in discomfort''

Aged 22 I set out to discover the world. Travelling via backpack and tent, I managed to traverse Eastern and Western Europe for 2 years on no more than the £700 in my pocket. Living on beaches, joining sustainable eco projects, and proving to myself that there was a magic to living life continually outside of the comfort zone. Life was bliss, until a deep sense of emptiness began to shadow my life. I was living my wildest dreams, refusing to settle into a high paid corporate ‘soul-less’ job and old age retirement, yet I lacked life prupose and meaning…

Aged 25 I hit an all time low in life. Addicted to sugar, marijuana and feeling extremely un-useful to the world, I contemplated suicide, and what it would be like to no longer have my live. ”What’s the point?” I asked, for I simply could not see value in my time and life.

''your life has purpose when you learn to live for something greater than your own desires''

I rebelled against such an idea during my early 20’s, however now I can see. In 2019 I began teaching Yoga & Meditation, and was surprised by how well I was able to help others create lasting change in their mental and physical health. People usually find it hard to believe when they experience silence of the mind for the first time. Especially those who had decided they ‘couldn’t meditate’. Many have stated to have their ‘mind blown’ after having experienced inner peace with such ease, my mother couldn’t believe it when I showed her how simple it could be. Yoga revealed my purpose, and that is to teach others how to self-mentor themselves through any mind-body challenge.

''let me be your guide''

Coming out of depression & the addiction to escaping from my emotions, I discovered my life’s purpose as a mentor and coach, helping you to uncover the roots of self-sabotaging cycles of behaviour, granting you the personal power to create the impact, life, & health you dream of.

If you’re struggling and feel like you’re constantly fighting against yourself, perhaps it’s time you take a leap towards the life you love to live. Make a change and take the first step today.

usman's bio

Usman Ali is the creator of SLFMASTERY, a new and exciting platform/podcast aiming to share, educate, inspire, and elevate the baseline level of human consciousness.

Usman has over a decade’s experience in the arts of Yoga, Meditaiton & Mindfulness and has now worked with countless individuals and several corporate clients like Sony Playstation and Merseyside Fire & Rescue Service.

Quoted by many as one of Liverpool’s best Yoga & Meditation teachers, Usman continues to develop his practice and walk the path of his own self-mastery. As Usman grows and develops, so does his capacity to show up as one of the new, young contributors to the wellness world.
